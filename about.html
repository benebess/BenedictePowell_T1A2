About me

My name is Benedicte Powell, or Bene for short.
...
I am a 28 year old currently studying at Coder Academy in Brisbane City.

I have been interested in computers from a very young age, which is what has led me to where I am now.
Since starting my Diploma of Information Technology I have found an even bigger love for all things computers, and find myself coding away even in my spare time.

In my spare time I enjoy an array of different activities, including, though not limited to; Designing posters, painting and drawing, playing video games and board games and of course, spending quality time with my wonderful partner and our delightful cat.
