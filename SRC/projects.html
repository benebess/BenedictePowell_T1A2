Poster Design for live bands

During lockdown last year I got back into photoshop and tried my hand at designing posters. I tried all sorts of things while getting some practise, including even making mock movie posters with my friends on them in place of celebrities! When the live music venue I worked at reopened and I went back to work, I noticed a lot of bands that played lacked a poster, noticing this gap, I decided to offer to make one for a band that had a gig coming up! It went really well and now I do it on the regular! I have thoroughly enjoyed the whole process and don't see myself stopping anytime soon!
